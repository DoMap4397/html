Scrambled Eggs

Egga are ones of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled fggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs.
  • 1tbs butter.
  • 2tbs cream.

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
